Key Features & Benefits

1. Aerospace-Grade Aluminum Construction

Built from precision-machined 7075-T6 aluminum, the Jowl Recovery Shackle delivers high tensile strength with minimal weight. This premium material ensures exceptional load capacity and corrosion resistance in extreme conditions.

2. Closed-System Safety Design

Unlike open hooks that can release under tension, this shackle’s closed design keeps recovery lines secure. It minimizes the risk of flying projectiles or accidental disconnection during high-stress recoveries.

3. Quick-Latch Mechanism

The innovative quick-connect system simplifies the recovery process by allowing one-handed operation. This feature saves time during recoveries and provides peace of mind when every second counts.

4. Flame-Red Anodized Finish

The bold red anodized surface adds more than just visual appeal, it resists fading, rust, and surface wear. It also helps users quickly identify recovery points in low-light or muddy conditions.

5. High Load Rating

Each Jowl Recovery Shackle is tested and rated to handle heavy recovery loads typical of off-road vehicles. This ensures reliable strength under extreme tension, helping drivers recover safely from deep mud, sand, or snow.

6. Universal Fit Compatibility

The shackle hook accommodates most recovery straps, winch lines, and soft shackles, making it a versatile addition to any recovery kit. It’s compatible with both synthetic and steel cables, ensuring flexibility in setup.

7. Noise-Reducing Isolator

A protective rubber isolator prevents metal-to-metal contact when mounted on a bumper or hitch. This reduces vibration noise and protects the shackle’s finish from scratches during travel.

8. Corrosion and Weather Resistance

From humid swamps to salty beaches, the anodized aluminum body and stainless steel pin resist corrosion. Users enjoy long-lasting durability without constant cleaning or maintenance.

9. Easy Maintenance

Unlike traditional shackles that rust or seize, the Jowl’s smooth anodized surface and quick-release pin make cleaning simple. A quick rinse removes dirt or debris, keeping it trail-ready at all times.

10. Lightweight but Strong

Despite its strength, this shackle weighs significantly less than steel alternatives. That means less strain on bumpers, mounts, and recovery bags, without sacrificing pulling power.
